House Call Policies

Please read before making an appointment — these keep everything smooth for both of us.

1. Book 24hrs in Advance

All house call appointments must be booked at least 24 hours ahead to guarantee your slot.

2. 2-Way Transport Fee

A 2-way transport fee is due by 6pm the day before your appointment. No exceptions.

3. Cancel 24hrs Ahead

Cancellations must be made 24 hours in advance. Exceptions for illness or unavoidable circumstances only.

4. Full Payment After Service

Full payment is due immediately after your service is completed.

5. Send Accurate Location

Please send your accurate location and clear directions before the appointment date.
